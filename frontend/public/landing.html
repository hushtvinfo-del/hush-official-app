Live TV · EPG

The guide. Finally.

A real program guide — horizontal timeline, 14 days of data, now-playing ribbon, and a Record button one click away on every show.

  • Red "now-playing" ribbon
    Scroll the timeline and the live moment moves with you. No more "wait, is this even live?"
  • One-click record
    A red Record chip sits in every live-player OSD. Press OK — it's queued to the cloud.
  • Real channel logos, everywhere
    Logos in the EPG, mini-EPG, now-playing chip and channel picker. No placeholder grey squares.
  • Auto-reconnect + "Back online" toast
    CDN hiccup, Wi-Fi drop, stalled decoder — the player self-heals and tells you when it's back.
20 HOURS · FREE · INCLUDED

Cloud DVR,
built in.

Every profile gets 20 hours of rolling cloud recording on a dedicated RAID-0 server. No disk to manage, no NAS to configure.

  • One-click Record from the EPG
    Tap the red chip on any live channel. Show ends? So does the recording — we read the EPG for you.
  • Full scrubber — rewind, fast-forward, seek
    MP4 moov-atom remuxed for instant seek. Completed recordings auto-play on open instead of pausing.
  • "My Recordings" on TV and mobile
    Settings → My Content → My Recordings. Quota bar, newest-first list, tap to play, long-press to delete.
  • Red "● REC" badges on live lists
    Glance at the EPG or browse screen and you know instantly what's recording right now.
  • Private by design
    Pseudonymous device hashes only — your IPTV credentials never leave the app. 14-day auto-retention.

Install & Update

Common questions.

How do I update if I'm already on HushTV?
Open the app — it'll detect v1.43.59 and offer an in-app update. Accept, and it reinstalls itself. If you miss the prompt, tap Install above and sideload manually.
Will my playlists and Continue Watching survive the update?
Yes. Updates preserve every playlist, PIN setting, layout preference, Continue Watching entry and My List pick.
How do I start my 30-day Hush+ trial?
Open the Hush+ tab inside the app. The trial starts automatically on your first visit — no card, no form. Existing Hush+ customers skip the trial and unlock full access instantly.
Do I need a separate subscription for Hush+?
Current Hush+ customers are already unlocked for life. Everyone else gets a full 30 days free — after that, Hush+ continues as a one-time add-on tied to your HushTV account.
Does Cloud DVR count against any usage quota?
20 hours of rolling cloud storage per profile, auto-trimmed at 14 days. Stopping early only counts the actual recorded seconds against your quota.
Which devices are supported?
Fire TV (all generations), Android TV, Nvidia Shield, Chromecast with Google TV, Android phones and tablets, foldables. One sideload, sign in once, pick up across every device.
